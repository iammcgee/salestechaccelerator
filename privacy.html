Last updated: November 2025

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to secure your information, no online transmission is completely risk-free.

We use cookies, pixels, and tracking technologies to measure website performance, track ad conversions, and enhance user experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

We may share limited information with trusted partners such as Calendly (for scheduling) or analytics platforms for performance tracking. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

6. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting info@getsalestech.com. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying “STOP.”

7. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. The most recent version will always be posted on this page.
